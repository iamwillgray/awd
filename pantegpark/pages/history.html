Full History

Honours of Panteg Park Bowls Club



1929

E.W. Morgan represents MBA against The Civil Service.



R. Whatmore snr. & S. Poulsom win EVBA Pairs Final.



1930

E.W. Morgan represents Wales Civil Service in the Civil Service Internationals at Southampton.



1932

E.W. Morgan represents Wales Civil Service in the Civil Service Internationals at Bournemouth.



1933

E.W. Morgan appointed Vice-Chairman of the MBA.



E.W. Morgan represents Wales Civil Service against The Royal Household Staff at Windsor Castle.



1934

E.W. Morgan appointed President of the MBA.



1935

A.H. Bailey represents the Wales Touring Team against The London & Southern Counties Team.



Club win the Pontypool Hospital Shield defeating Garndiffaith Musical Club by 106 to 72.



1938

E.W. Morgan represents Wales Civil Service in the Civil Service Internationals in Scotland.



1942

Club wins the Eastern Valley League Cup defeating Talywain.



Club wins the Pontypool Hospital Shield defeating Talywain 82 – 65.



1943

W.F. Lewis appointed President of the Eastern Valley League.



1945

Club wins the MBA Club Competition beating Bedwellty Park 96 – 82 at Newbridge Bowls Club.



Club runners-up in the Pontypool Hospital Shield Competition.



1947

E.W. Morgan appointed Executive Member of the MBA.



Club wins the Pontypool Hospital Shield beating Pontnewydd 100 – 69.



1949

E.W. Morgan made a Life-Member of the MBA.



1950

W. Moses, W.T. Morgan, S. Tasker & J. Collier win the Eastern Valley League Fours Competition.



1951

O. Benjamin wins the Eastern Valley League Singles.



1953

New pavilion opened in May.



E.W. Morgan appointed to the WBA Selection Committee & treasurer of the MBA.



1954

Club wins the Eastern Valley League beating Pontnewydd 96 – 65.



1955

J. Pipe & W. Moses win Eastern Valley League Pairs Competition.



1956

W. Lawley wins the Eastern Valley League Singles Competition beating J. Perry of ROF by 21 – 12.



J. Pipe & W. Moses win the Eastern Valley Pairs Competition.



1957

J. Pipe & W. Moses win the Eastern Valley Pairs Competition.



J. Pipe wins the Newport Athletic Cup Open Singles.



J. Pipe, W. Moses & A. Whatmore win the Triples Competition at Clevedon.



Club wins the Eastern Valley League defeating Cwmbran by 13 shots.



1958

J. Pipe wins the Festival of Wales Singles Trophy beating English International H.F. Etheridge 21 – 18 at Llandrindod Wells.



J. Pipe & W. Lawley win the Pairs Trophy (Unofficial Welsh Championship) beating Welsh Internationals R.F. Evans & D.L. Williams 20 – 13.



W. Lawley wins the Eastern Valley League Singles Competition beating H. Owen (Talywain) 21 – 17.



J. Pipe & W. Moses win the Eastern Valley Pairs competition beating A. Lloyd & C. Jones (Panteg House) 28 – 11.



Club wins the Eastern Valley League beating Pontnewydd 96 – 66.



1959

J. Pipe & W. Moses win MBA Pairs Competition beating pair from Brynmawr 23 – 13.



W. Lawley wins Eastern Valley League Singles beating C. Jones (Panteg House) 21 – 19.



Club wins the Eastern Valley League beating Pontnewydd 88 – 84.



J. Pipe wins The Spa Cup at Llandrindod Wells.



1960

J. Pipe wins The Spa Cup at Llandrindod Wells.



W. Lawley wins the Eastern Valley Bowling Assoc. Singles Competition beating T. Regan (Blaenavon) 21 – 10.



Rev. M. Davies, A. Tibbs, A. Campbell & S. Poulsom win the Eastern Valley Bowling Assoc.Fours Competition.



J. Pipe & W. Moses win the EVBA Pairs Competition.



Club wins the EVBA League Championship.



1961

J. Pipe & W. Moses win the Open Pairs Competition in Weston-Super- Mare Tournament.



A. Lloyd wins the EVBA Singles Competition beating W. Betts (ROF) 21 – 18.



J. Pipe & W. Moses win the EVBA Pairs Competition.



1962

J. Pipe wins the Newport Athletic Club Tournament Open Singles.



J. Pipe & W. Moses win the EVBA Pairs Title.



Rev. M. Davies, A. Campbell, B. Rogers & S. Poulsom win the EVBA Fours Title.



1963

W. Lawley wins the MBA Singles Title beating A. Jones (Cross Keys) 21 – 15.



J. Pipe wins the Glyn Ebbw Tournament beating team mate I. Meyrick 21 – 12.



J. Pipe wins the EVBA Singles Title beating S. Price (Girlings) 21 – 12.



1964

J. Pipe selected for British Isles Bowls Team to tour Australia.



J. Pipe selected for Wales Indoor Bowls Team.



J. Pipe selected for Wales for Home Championships in Belfast.



J. Pipe wins EVBA Singles Title beating team mate B. Rogers 21 – 18.



J. Pipe & W. Moses win EVBA Pairs Title beating team mates W. Lawley & I. Meyrick 23 – 17.



I. Evans, H. Thompson, G. Watkins & A. Campbell win EVBA Fours Title.



Club wins the EVBA Team Championship beating Pontnewydd 106 – 68.



1965

S. Tasker elected EVBA President.



A. Campbell elected EVBA Secretary.



Club wins EVBA Team Championship.



1966

J. Pipe selected for Wales in World Championships in Australia.



Club wins EVBA Team Championship.



1967

Club wins EVBA Team Championship.



1968

A. Campbell & S. Tasker win EVBA Pairs Title beating team mates J. Hazard & W.R. Jones.



1969

S. Tasker becomes Life Member of EVBA.
