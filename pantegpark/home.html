About the club

Panteg Park Bowls Club was established in 1927 and the green is located at the Fishponds Park, Cwrdy Road, Griffithstown NP4 5LX. We are affiliated to the WBA, MBA, and EVBA and we can also accomodate friendlies as required. We are Currently in division 5 of the Monmouthshire Bowling Association League, and play mid week matches in the EVBA league.

Come join in

Once you have tried the game you will be bowled over. We are a very proud and friendly club and operate with a small but dedicated membership and would welcome every one of all ages to join the club, either experienced bowlers or those who wish to take up the game. No experience is required and all current members would be more than happy to assist anyone who required help or tuition. If you would like to join the club, please contact us.

Honours

Panteg Park Bowls Club are also very proud and honoured to have had a long standing life member Roger Jones, who won the Welsh National Open Singles Championship in 2008 and the British Isles Championship in 2009 which was held in Ireland. Also Welsh International 2012.
